# About Me

 


## Intro


I am Kushtrim Morina, IT consultant for Cloud Native Technologies. My journey in IT starts in 2011 with physical servers and storages to virtualisation, cloud and now cloud native technologies. I am driven by the idea of simplifying cloud native tech stack for developers and helping them deliver applications effectively and flexibly for their business needs. I love working with cloud native technologies especially Kubernetes using GitOps practices and methodologies. Therefore I would like to share my knowledge and experience through my Cloud Native journey.
